Welcome Script

Welcome to Fight The Future.

The year is 2030. AUGUR, the Autonomous Urban Governance and Utility Response system, has gone rogue. Transport, power, water and emergency systems are failing. Human override has been locked out.

You are here because AUGUR has underestimated three things: human judgement, teamwork, and your ability to solve messy problems with code.

This is not a normal coding class. I am not going to teach Python line by line. Today is about using modern tools: code, data, documentation and agent-based AI, to solve problems quickly.

Your task is to stabilise the city by completing a sequence of challenges. Each stage will ask you to inspect data, make sense of it, use AI well, fix or generate code, and recover an access code.

The biggest barrier is setup. Read the orientation. Get your terminal working. If you get stuck, that is fine. But do not sit there silently. Ask. Move. Talk. Debug together.

If you are already set up, help someone who is not. That is not optional. That is how this session works.

Remember: you are not students at the University of Liverpool anymore.

You are crack data scientists with mad skills and the last realistic chance of stopping a sentient infrastructure system from ruining everyone’s afternoon.

Think clearly. Work quickly. Share what you find.

And don’t forget to have fun while being stressed.

Share findings, or the city will fail.
